Cards and how to play them

Main Monsters

Main Monsters arae the heart and soul of any deck, they are your companion as you face off against other Main Monsters. Each Main Monster has Health, Attack, Effect, and a Mana. Your Main Monster cannot be destroyed by effects Also your Main Monsters mana gets reset durning the start of your turn. Main Monsters cannot do Tasks.

Mana

Mana is what allows you to activate/summon other cards. Examples include: Protection, Equip, and Monsters.

Protection

Protection cards help your main monster, gain Attack, gain Health, reduce Attack from your opponet, Shuffle your deck, Draw cards, negate other cards, Etc. Protection cards can be played at anytime aslong as you have mana.

Equip

Equip cards attach to your Main Monster to boost or gain strength. Equip cards can only be played on your turn.

Monsters

Monsters unlike main monsters dont have a effect and use mana to summon them. These cards can Attack and do tasks. If a Monster is doing a task it cannot attack, and if a Monster attacks it cannot do tasks

Tasks

Task cards unlike other cards, dont use mana to play them. But they need Monsters to complete them before activating their effects.

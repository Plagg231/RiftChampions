Remember this game isnt so complicated, just remember the first 3 rules and have fun

Rules are . . .

  1. If your opponents Main monster has 0 health, you win
  2. When you or your opponent run out of cards in your main deck you lose
  3. You must always have 3 cards in hand
    • If you have less than 3 cards in hand, draw cards intel you have 3
    • if you have more than 3 cards in hand, discard cards intel you have 3
  4. You can always forfit a game, if you do not want to play, the winner is the one who didnt forfit
  5. Both players must have 20 cards in their Main deck, The Main monster does not count as a Main deck card
  6. When you or your opponet activates a card. You can chain a card in responce, but so can your opponent to your responce, as long as each player has mana to play the respective card(s).
  7. If you dont have enough mana to play a card, you cannot play that card
  8. The turn players Main Monster must attack to end their turn, unless otherwise
  9. During your turn you can play Protection, Equip, Monsters, and Tasks, but for your opponents turn you can only activate Protection cards.

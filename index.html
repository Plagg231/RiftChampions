Dev Mystics, presents Rift Champions

Rift Champions is a 2 player strategy game were you and you opponent fight with your main monsters to see which champion competes next.

Story

Within the depths of space, there was a great Universal Rift. A tear in the fabric of time and reality. Over time this Universal Rift became large enough for interlopers to travel through. This would eventually lead to a power struggle for control over the Universeal Rift.

Through time different societys sent out their champions for fight in this great conflict was known as the War of the Rift.

Do you have what it takes to be RIFT CHAMPION?

Warning: this game inst avalible for the public, this game is still in the development stage, and we hope to make a mobile version of the game later on. Sorry for any misleading text.
